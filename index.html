Johann Maldonado

Web Designer and Classical Singer

Born in Caracas, Venezuela. Classical Singer (Baritone), Celta-Qualified English Language Teacher and Web Design learner. Current member of the Simón Bolívar National Choir and Camerata Barroca de Caracas. Former member of the Teatro Teresa Carreño's Opera Choir. He began studies at the Escuela de Música de Chacao and continued his studies at Escuela Superior de Música "José Ángel Lamas" and Escuela de Música "Juan Manuel Olivares", obtaining his degree as Classical Singer by the lead of his tutor Roy Holder.

He has taken part in many Opera performances, both as a soloist and choirist. Among his roles he has performed as "Leporello" in Mozart's Don Giovanni and as "Cristopher Columbus" in the celebrated Venezuelan Opera "Los Martirios de Colón".

He has also attended masterclasses with celebrated musicians as could be Johannes Kammler, Pedro Liendo, Susana Frangi, Vittorio Terranova, Bernhard Kerres; Acting and performance lessons with Marco Gandini, Miguel Issa, Dimas Gonzáles and Israel Moreno.

He has also had the ooportunity to take part in many performances in France, Italy, United Kingdom, Portugal, and Austria under the leading conduction of Gustavo Dudamel and Lourdes Sánchez and in Venezuela with Todd Harper, Josep Vila, Julien Salemkour, among renowned others.
